Bakmie Ayam Karet

Description

Growing up on the west side of jakarta made me appreciate the abundance of good Bakmie stalls and restaurant, especially when you like them as much as i do. Here's a recipe to one of my favorite Bakmie.

Ingredients

The Noodle

  • 300 Gram of rubber noodles
  • Water enough to boil noodles

The Chicken

  • A generous amount of water
  • 250 Gram chicken breast
  • 1 teaspoon whole black pepper
  • 3 Cloves garlic, chopped
  • 1/2 clove onion, chopped
  • 3 Stalks spring onions, thinly sliced

Stir Fried Chicken Toppings

  • 1 clove garlic, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon pepper
  • Sufficient cooking oil

Noodle Seasoning

  • Chicken oil to taste (1 tbsp per bowl)
  • Soy sauce to taste (1 tbsp per bowl)
  • Oyster sauce to taste (1 tsp per bowl)
  • White pepper to taste (1 tsp per bowl)

Instructions

Chicken toppings

  1. Heat plenty of water in a pan until it boils. Add whole black pepper, chopped garlic and chopped onions, as well as sliced green onions. Stir until evenly mixed.
  2. Once done, add the chicken to the boiling spiced water. Cover the pan and simmer for approximately 30 minutes.
  3. After 30 minutes, turn off the heat, but don't remove it immediately. Let the chicken rest for another 30 minutes to allow the spices to fully absorb.
  4. Once done, remove from heat and drain briefly. Then, slice or shred the chicken into small pieces. Set the chicken aside.
  5. Next, pour a little oil into a frying pan and heat it. Sauté the chopped garlic until fragrant. Then, add the chicken, which has been cut into small pieces.
  6. Season with fish sauce, soy sauce, oyster sauce, and sesame oil. Don't forget to add salt and pepper to taste. Stir well and sauté until cooked through. Set aside!

The Noodles

  1. Heat water in a saucepan until boiling. Add the rubber noodles and cook, stirring frequently, until cooked through.
  2. While waiting for the rubber noodles to cook, prepare the bowl. Pour the chicken oil, soy sauce, oyster sauce, and a little ground pepper into the bowl. Stir until evenly mixed.
